## The Art of Counting ğŸ§®

**Simple Method:**

-   Inhale: "One"
-   Exhale: (silent)
-   Inhale: "Two"
-   Continue to 10, then start over

**When you lose count:**

-   No judgment
-   Simply return to "One"
-   This IS the practice

## Working with Difficulties ğŸŒŠ

### **Anxiety or Racing Thoughts**

-   Longer exhales (6-8 counts)

-   "Breathing out tension"

### **Drowsiness**

-   Sit more upright
-   Eyes slightly more open
-   Focus on inhale

### **Restlessness**

-   Count breaths backward: 10-1
-   Very gentle noting: "thinking"
-   Return to breath anchor
